Workout of the Day
Warmup
Back Squats (41X0) 3 sets x 5 reps with dynamic stretches between each set.
After Back Squats, warm up Pull Ups.
Strength
4 sets:
A1. Back Squat (41X0) x 4-5 reps (rest 20 seconds)
A2. Pull Ups x Max Reps (rest 3 minutes)
Conditioning
AMRAP in 12 minutes:Max HSPU or Push UpRun 200 meters
Post rounds and reps to comments.
Scaling:
Use bands for pullups. No negatives for HSPU. If unable to perform HSPU, sub push ups.
