Workout of the Day
Warmup
2 sets of entire complex w/ empty barbell
+
1 set of entire complex with weight added (determine starting weight for workout)
Training
5 sets for max weight:
3 rounds of 2 Power Cleans2 Hang Squat Cleans2 Front Squats

Goal is to complete all 3 rounds without resting the bar on the ground with as much weight as possible
If you are successful, you MUST increase weight on the next set
If you fail and rest the bar on the ground, finish the set and stay at that weight for the next set
Rest 3-4 minutes b/w sets

Post heaviest successful set to comments.
