Benchmark
"Lynne"
Five rounds for max reps of:Body weight Bench PressPull-ups
Take no rest between Bench Press and Pullups. Rest 3-4 minutes after Pullups.
Goal is max reps. Record each round and post to comments.
Scaling
Bench Press:

If you cannot Bench Press your bodyweight, scale to a weight that allows you to complete 15-20 reps on your first set.

Pullups:

If you can complete 10 or more Pullups: Perform as RX'd
If you can complete 1-9 Pullups: Perform as many Pullups as possible in 1 minute (break up into as many sets as needed)
If you cannot perform Pullups: Use as light a band as possible to complete 10-15 reps in your first set
