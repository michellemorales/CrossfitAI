Workout of the Day
In teams of 2:
5 sets:Partner 1 Row 400 metersPartner 2 Max Burpee Pullups
Pullup Bar should be 12" above max reach.
Each partner will row a total of 2000 meters. While Partner 1 is rowing their 400 meter piece, Partner 2 is performing as many Burpee Pullups as possible. Rotate and repeat for 5 sets each.
