Workout of the Day
CF Group Class
In teams of four, rotate through 10 minutes at each station:
Station 1:
As a team, row as many meters as possible.Teammates must rotate every 250 meters.
Station 2:
Prowler Push across the gym, as many times as possible.
Men use low handles, women use high handles. Teammates must rotate upon finishing each push across the gym.
Station 3:
Rope climb, as many times as possible. Teammates must rotate every rep. Station 4:Bear Crawl twice across gym, as many times as possible.Teammmates must rotate upon completion of bear crawling back and forth across the gym.Note: Every non-working team member will hold a 10lb or 15lb plate in each hand throughout the duration of the workout.
