Strength
Snatch Grip Deadlift (51X0 tempo)In 20 minutes complete 4-5 tough sets of 5
Perfect technique. Chest up, hips down in setup. Squeeze bar off of floor (no jerking). Hips and shoulders rise together. Bar kisses floor on negative. No break in posture at all.
Conditioning
30 Man Makers for time. (30# DBs/20# DBs)
Stand with a DB in each, place DBs on floor and kick feet back into pushup position, drop into push up, push into top of push up  position, perform a 1 arm row per hand where the elbow travels much  higher than the back; kick legs in, perform a squat clean thruster.
If you cannot perform the pushup from the feet, drop to your knees, perform the pushup, then return to your feet for the DB rows.
