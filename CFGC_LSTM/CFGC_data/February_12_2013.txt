Strength
Push Press (21X1 tempo)5 sets of 3-5 reps
Start at 70% of your 1RM and increase each setThe goal is to perform 5 reps each setIf you can get 5 reps, increase weight on the next setIf you can get 4 reps, increase weight on the next setIf you only get 3 reps, stay at that weight on the next set
