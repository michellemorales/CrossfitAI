Run 800 metersAMRAP Wall Balls
Run 800 meters AMRAP Rope Climbs/Modified Rope Climbs
Two members of the team head out for an 800 meter run. The other 2 team members complete AMRAP Wall Balls with only one person working at a time. When BOTH team members return from the run, you switch places.
After all 4 team members have completed the first 800 meter run, you repeat the same format, but with Rope Climbs.
