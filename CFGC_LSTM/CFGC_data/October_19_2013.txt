Workout of the Day
In teams of 2 complete:60 Burpees to Plate60 Pullups120 Plate Ground to Overhead (45/35)60 Pullups60 Burpees to Plate
Divide the work in half and partition as needed. Finish all of the Burpees before moving onto the Pullups, etc.
Scale Pullup reps according to ability.
If unable to perform Pullups, sub Toes to Bar/Knees to Elbows.
