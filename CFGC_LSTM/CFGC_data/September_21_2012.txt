Workout of the Day
Warmup
3 sets:5 x Hang Power Snatch5 x OH Squat5 x Snatch Drop5 x Hang Squat Snatch
Start with PVC pipe and move to an empty barbell on sets 2&3
Dynamic Stretches b/w sets
Strength
Hang Snatch 3-3-3-3-3-3
Goal is to increase weight each set - only increase weight if you are able to receive the bar in a full OH Squat with solid positioning and mechanicsIf unable to load the OH Squat position, perform Power Snatches and receive the bar as low as possible.
Conditioning
Climb the ladder as high as possible in 12 minutes:Wall Ball x 2,4,6,8,10,12...Toes to Bar x 1,2,3,4,5,6...
Post 3RM from Part 1 and last set/rep completed from Part 2 to comments.
