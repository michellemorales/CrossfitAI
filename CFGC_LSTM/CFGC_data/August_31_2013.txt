Workout of the Day
In teams of 2 complete,
3 rounds for time:
100 Plate Ground to Overhead (45/35)2 x 200 meter run each (relay style)
Break up the 100 Plate Ground to Overhead however you like. While one partner is working, the other is resting.
Each partner runs two 200 meter runs each round. While one partner is running, the other is resting.
