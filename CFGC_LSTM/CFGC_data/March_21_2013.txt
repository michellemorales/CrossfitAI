Workout of the Day
Warmup
Spend 5-10 minutes performing Dynamic Stretches+
3 sets:8-10 Wall Balls20-30 Double Unders/Double Under practice
+Warmup/practice Muscle Ups, Pullups or Toes to Bar (whichever you're doing in workout)
CrossFit Games Open 13.3
Complete as many rounds and reps as possible in 12 minutes of: 150 Wall ball shots, (Men: 20#, 10' Target. Women: 14#, 9' Target) 90 Double-unders 30 Muscle-ups
For a downloadable PDF of the workout, click here.
Scaling Options:
CF Games Open Competitors:
No scaling. Perform as RX'd and get through as much of workout as possible in 12 minutes.
Non-Competitors:
Wall Ball: Scale weight to allow you to hit target for ALL reps. Scale reps to max in 8 minutes.
Double Unders: No sub. Scale reps to 20, 40 or 60 reps according to ability level.
Muscle Ups: Scale to max Pullups or Knees to Elbows in remaining time.
