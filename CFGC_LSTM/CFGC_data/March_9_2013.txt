Workout of the Day
With a partner perform AMRAP sets in 20 minutes of:
15 KB Swings (heaviest possible - all sets unbroken)10 Box Jump Overs (24/20)1 Wall Climb
Partner 1 completes one full set while Partner 2 rests.
As soon as Partner 1 completes their Wall Climb, Partner 2 begins their set while Partner 1 rests.
Perform each set as fast as possible.
