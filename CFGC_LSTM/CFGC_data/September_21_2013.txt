Workout of the Day
In teams of 2:
Run 800 metersAMRAP 10 Alternating DB Snatches (55/35), 10 Hand Release PushupsRun 800 metersAMRAP 1 Rope Climb, 15 Wall Balls (20/14)
While Partner 1 runs 800 meters, Partner 2 completes AMRAP DB Snatch/Pushups. Once Partner 1 returns, switch places. Repeat for the second run. 
Notes/Scaling:
DB Snatches are 10 reps total - 5 per arm each set
Sub for Rope Climbs is 2 modified Rope Climbs
