Workout of the Day
Warmup
2 sets with empty barbell:5 Hang Power Cleans5 Front Squats5 Hang Squat Cleans
Dynamic stretches b/w sets
Prep
3 sets of3 Hang Squat Cleans
Build to starting weight for workout
Training
Every minute on the minute for 20 minutes:3 Hang Squat Cleans

Add weight (5-10% depending on difficulty) every 5 minutes.
If you fail, attempt that weight again on the next minute.
If you fail a weight twice, drop down to your previous weight.
Last 5 minutes, go as heavy as possible.

Post heaviest successful set to comments.
