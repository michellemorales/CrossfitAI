Workout of the Day
Warmup
3 sets w/ your workout partner:5 Deadlifts (increase weight each set)10 KB Swings5 Groiners
Training
In teams of 2 complete the following three stations:
1. In 15 minutes:
Each partner build to a 1 rep max Deadlift
(Rest 5 minutes)
2. In 10 minutes:
Complete as many KB Swings (32kg/20kg) as possible - only one partner working at a time
(Rest 5 minutes)
3. In 5 minutes:
Complete as many Burpees as possible - only one partner working at a time
Total score = Sum of each partner's 1RM Deadlift + total KB Swings + total Burpees
Post total score to comments.
