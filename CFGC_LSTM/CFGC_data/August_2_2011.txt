Workout of the Day
Warmup:
CFWU x 2
Replace pullups with 5 Skin the Cats
Strength:
Weighted Pullups (41X0 tempo)
3-2-1-3-2-1
Second wave heavier than first.
Conditioning:
In 12 minutes climb the following ladder as high as possible:1 Sumo Deadlift High Pull (95/65)1 Push Press (95/65)2 Sumo Deadlift High Pull2 Push Press3 Sumo Deadlift High Pull3 Push Press4,5,6...
Post heaviest weighted pullup and time to comments.
Scaling:
Progression for Pullups (from most to least advanced):

Chest to Bar Weighted Pullups
Chin Over Bar Weighted Pullups
Weighted Negatives (5 second negative for 5 sets of 5 reps)
Bodyweight Negatives (5 second negative for 5 sets of 5 reps)
Single Leg Assisted Pullups (50X1 tempo x 5 reps x 5 sets)
Double Leg Assisted Pullups (50X1 tempo x 5 reps x 5 sets)

For Assisted Pullups, use as little assistance from legs as possible.
