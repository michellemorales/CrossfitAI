"Mainsite Sunday"
CrossFit.com WOD from 01/13/13:
"Lynne"
Five rounds for max reps of: Body weight bench press Pull-ups
Take no rest between Bench Press and Pullups. Rest 3-4 minutes after Pullups.
Goal is max reps. Record each round in your notebooks and post to comments.
Rich Froning: 20/22, 20/20, 19/20, 18/20, 17/20 (195lb bench/strict pull-ups).
WOD Demo with Copper Mountain CrossFit - video [wmv] [mov] [HD mov]
Scaling:
Bench Press: If you cannot Bench Press your bodyweight, scale to a    weight that allows you to complete 15-20 reps on your first set.
Pullups:

If you can complete 10 or more Pullups: Perform as RX'd
If you can complete 1-9 Pullups: Perform as many Pullups as possible in 1 minute (break up into as many sets as needed)
If you cannot perform Pullups: Use as light a band as possible to complete 10-15 reps in your first set
