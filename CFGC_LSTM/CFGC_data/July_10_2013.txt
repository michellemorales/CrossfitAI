Workout of the Day
Warmup
Lax Ball - Shoulder Circuit on Floor
+
15 OHS (PVC, move hands & feet closer every 5 reps)
+
2 sets w/ empty barbell:5 x Push Press5 x Front Squat
Strength
Push Press (21X2)3-3-3-3-3-3
Sets 1-3: Warmup sets (50-70% 1RM)Sets 4-6: Working sets (80-90% 1RM)
Hold to top position for 2 full seconds.
Conditioning
8 sets of Alternating Tabata:Push Press (75/55)Front Squat (75/55)
Perform 20 seconds of Push Press followed by 10 seconds of rest. Perform 20 seconds of Front Squat followed by 10 seconds of rest. Continue in this fashion for 8 sets of each.
Scale weight to a "light" barbell. The weight should not be a limiting factor.
Post 3RM and lowest round of Push Press & Front Squat to comments.
