Workout of the Day
In teams of 2 complete AMRAP in 12 minutes of:
5 Power Cleans5 Thrusters5 Burpees
Partner 1 completes a full set, then Partner 2 completes a full set...perform as many sets as possible in 12 minutes.
Men's Weight: 135#Women's Weight: 95#
Scale accordingly - sets should be unbroken.
If you and your partner cannot use the same weight, set up 2 barbells.
