Workout of the Day
Warmup:
10 Front Squats (Empty Barbell)Shoulder Mob - Lax Ball
10 Front Squats (Add weight)Dynamic Stretches - Lower Body
10 Front Squats (Add more weight)Dynamic Stretches - Lower Body
Strength:
Front Squat (40X1)
Build to a heavy set of 3 reps in 20 minutes
NO FAILED REPS. Emphasis on tempo, depth and technique.
Conditioning:
"Death by Thruster" (95/65)
Perform 1 Thruster the 1st minute, 2 Thrusters the 2nd minute, continuing for as long as you are able.
Scaling:
Use 30-40% of your 3 rep max Front Squat.
