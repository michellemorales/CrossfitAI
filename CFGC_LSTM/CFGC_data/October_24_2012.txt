Workout of the Day
Warmup
Open Gym for 20 minutes
Work toward accomplishing something you have yet to achieve.
Some possibilities:

Rope Climb
Muscle Up
Pull Up
Handstand/HSPU
Double Unders

If you have already accomplished all of these, spend time working on the 1 or 2 that need the most work.
Training

Sets 2 & 4:
On a 3 minute timer complete:Run 400 meters as fast as possibleMax KB Swings (32kg/20kg) in remaining time
Rest 3 minutes between sets
