Workout of the Day
Warmup:
CFWU x 3
Perform 3-5 skin the cats or practice climbing the rope instead of pullups
Training:
For time:
150 KB Swings (24kg/16kg)
Every time you stop swinging or put the KB down, perform Rope Climbs (men: 2, women: 1)
Perform the hardest version of the rope climb possible (L-sit, legless, etc.)
KB Swings are overhead
Scaling:
If unable to climb the rope, perform 5 supine rope climbs (lay on floor, pull hand over hand to standing)
