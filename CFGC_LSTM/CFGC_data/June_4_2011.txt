Workout of the Day
Teams of 3, everyone must run together at the beginning and the end.  Only one person working at a time on the other exercises, with the  remaining 2 athletes taking turns holding a 25# plate overhead (the plate  must be overhead the entire time during the Box Jumps, Push Press, Air  Squats, and Walking Lunges).
For time:

 Run 400 meters (entire team)
 150 Box Jumps (24"/18")
 75 Push Press (45#/25# dumbbells)
 75 Air Squats
 150 Steps Walking Lunge (with 45#/25# dumbbells)
 Run 400 meters (entire team)
