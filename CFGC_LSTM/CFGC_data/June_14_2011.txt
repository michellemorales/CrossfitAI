Workout of the Day
Warmup:
3 sets of 10 OH Squats (Barbell)Dynamic stretches b/w each set
Strength:
Press (21X2 tempo)
Build to a heavy double in 15 minutes
Benchmark!
"Fran"
21-15-9 reps ofThrusters (95/65)Pullups
Scaling:
Thrusters: Choose weight that allows workout to be completed in under  10 minutes. If front rack or overhead position are undeveloped, use  DBs.
Pullups: Band assisted pullups. Choose band that allows for workout  to be completed in under 10 minutes. If you cannot get your chin over  the bar with the green band, perform Jumping Pullups (double the reps).
