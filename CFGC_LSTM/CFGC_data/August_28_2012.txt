Workout of the Day
Warmup
3 sets:
4 Power Cleans 3 Hang Squat Cleans 2 Front Squats 1 Push Jerk
Dynamic stretches b/w sets.
Start w/ empty barbell and add weight for next 2 warmup sets.
Determine weight for Set 1 of workout.
Training
6 sets for max weight:
4 Power Cleans3 Hang Squat Cleans2 Front Squats1 Push Jerk
Goal is to perform all 10 reps unbroken with as much weight as possible.
Rest 2-3 minutes b/w sets.
If you complete a set unbroken, you MUST increase weight on the next set.
Post heaviest unbroken set to comments.
