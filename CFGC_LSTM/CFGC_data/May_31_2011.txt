Workout of the Day:
Warmup:
Dynamic Stretches
Strength:
Press (21X2 tempo)
5,4,3,5,4,3
Rest 2 minutes b/w sets
The second wave of 5,4,3 should be heavier than the first wave.
Conditioning:
21-15-9 reps:Push Press (95/65)KB Swing (32k/24k)Box Jump (24"/18")
Notes/Scaling:
Push Press: Bar starts on ground. Scale weight to 60% of Press 3RM.
KB Swing: Overhead swings. Scale weight accordingly.
Box Jump: Standard is stand up to full hip/knee extension with both feet still on box.
