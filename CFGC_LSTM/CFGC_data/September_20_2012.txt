Workout of the Day
Warmup
3 sets w/ empty barbell:6 x Thruster4 x Front Squat2 x Push Jerk
2 minutes Double Under practice b/w sets (2 sets)(If proficient, perform 30 sec on/30 sec x 2)
Strength
3 x Thruster/2 x Front Squat/1 x Push Jerk
Build up to a max set in 15 minutes
Perform 4-5 sets of increasing weight
Conditioning
3 rounds for time:6 x Thruster4 x Front Squat2 x Push Press15 x Box Jump (24/20)
Weight = 75% of heaviest set from Part 1.
Post max from Part 1 and time/weight from Part 2 to comments.
