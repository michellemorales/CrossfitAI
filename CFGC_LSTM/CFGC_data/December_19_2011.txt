3 sets: Bench Press x 5-6 reps (build up to weight for workout)Pullups x 5-6 reps
Benchmark!
"Lynne"
5 rounds:Bodyweight Bench Press, Max RepsPullups, Max Reps
Take no rest between Bench Press and Pullups. Rest 3-4 minutes after Pullups.
Goal is max reps. Record each round in your notebooks and post to comments.
Scaling:
Bench Press: If you cannot Bench Press your bodyweight, scale to a  weight that allows you to complete 10-15 reps on your first set.
Pullups: If you cannot complete more than 5 pullups, use an  assistance band. Choose a band thickness that allows you to complete  10-15 reps on your first set.
