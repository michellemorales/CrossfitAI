Workout of the Day
Warmup:
Pick one Pull movement and one Push movement and complete 4-5 alternating sets of each in 30 minutes.
Pull: Muscle Up, Rope Climb, Pullup (Weighted, Chest to Bar, Kipping, Strict, Negatives, etc.)
Push: Muscle Up, HSPU, Ring Dips, Pushups
Sets should not be to failure. Focus on quality reps, full range of motion and challenging progressions.
Stretch hips, hamstrings, quads, calves, etc. b/w sets.
Benchmark!
