Workout of the Day
Warmup:
CFWU x 1
Workout Prep:
Determine appropriate Pistol progression for workout.
Determine appropriate scale for Ring Dips.
Training:
5 rounds:1 minute AMRAP Rowing (meters)1 minute AMRAP Ring Dips1 minute AMRAP Pistols1 minute Rest
Scaling:
